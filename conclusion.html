Summary of Differences

Model-based Evaluation: What Each Model Explains vs. Predicts
Model Type What it helps with predictions
Norman’s Cognitive / Descriptive Helps user go through a whole
chain of execution and evaluation. 		Not predictive model.
GOMS Analytical / Predictive Breaks a task into goals, methods,
and operators to understand how an expert user performs tasks. 		Estimate and compare the
efficiency of varying task methods.
KLM Quantitative / Predictive Focuses on basic physical and mental
actions such as keystrokes, pointing, and preparation. 		Average/Estimated time
for completion.

General Summary after understanding the three models -:

After using my phone's camera, I was able to put the test all three models and came up with a conclusion that Norman’s model of HCI helps a person to really visualize each step about to be taken when they want to reach their Goal.GOMS model will now help a person be efficient in their decision with what method has the least steps or is easier to achieve. Finally we can use KLM as well to estimate the time and try to switch methods/operators in order to reach the Goal in the shortest time possible.
Rather than using each model seperately, we could combine their benefits to compliment each other and achieve the best outcomes.
